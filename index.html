About Me

Hi there! I'm a Ruby on Rails and JavaScript developer with a background in math and physics. After developing software while I was in college for the High Energy Physics Group at Rutgers, I spent several years as a physics teacher. Once I was tenured I decided to start teaching some of my students programming after school, and then started teaching myself even more. I have recently completed App Academy's 12 week web development immersive bootcamp, coding and building new projects daily, and learning best practices in a challenging and collaborative environment.
